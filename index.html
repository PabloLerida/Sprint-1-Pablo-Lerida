Grandma's Secret Apple Pie Recipe

The Apple pie recipe has been in my family for generations and never fails to delight.

Ingredientes

  • 1/2 cup sugar.
  • 2tsp ground cinnamon.
  • 6 medium-sized apples, peeled and sliced.

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375ºF (190 ºC).
  2. In a bowl, mix the sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture.
